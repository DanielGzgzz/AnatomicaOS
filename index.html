Academic Bibliography & Engine Mechanisms

Scientific literature underpinning the Anatomica OS calculations.

  • Mifflin-St Jeor Equation: Mifflin, M. D., et al. (1990). A new predictive equation for resting energy expenditure in healthy individuals. The American Journal of Clinical Nutrition, 51(2), 241-247.
  • Tri-Phasic Rest Protocols: Zatsiorsky, V. M., & Kraemer, W. J. (2006). Science and Practice of Strength Training. Human Kinetics. (Basis for 24-72h recovery periods based on CNS vs structural loads).
  • Weight Loss Satiety & Protein Leverage: Simpson, S. J., & Raubenheimer, D. (2005). Obesity: the protein leverage hypothesis. Obesity Reviews, 6(2), 133-142. (Utilized for low-calorie, high-protein food combining).
  • Micronutrient Synergies (The Juicer): Combs, G. F., & McClung, J. P. (2016). The Vitamins: Fundamental Aspects in Nutrition and Health. Academic Press. (Mechanism for Vitamin D3 + Fat absorption, Calcium + Fortified Dairy deficits).
